The Girl by the Window
Edvard Munch
1893
Oil on canvas
96.5 × 65.4 cm (38 × 25 3/4 in.); Framed: 110.5 × 79.4 × 8.9 cm (43 1/2 × 31 1/4 × 3 1/2 in.)

Edvard Munch’s The Girl by the Window combines his exposure to Impressionism and Symbolism with his ongoing exploration of Romantic themes, such as isolation and inner turmoil. The painting’s mysterious, melancholic atmosphere is created through shadowy tones and a symbolic window that separates the girl from the outside world, deepening the sense of unease and unspoken tension.
