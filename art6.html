American Gothic
Grant Wood
1930
Oil on Beaver Board
78 × 65.3 cm (30 3/4 × 25 3/4 in.)

Grant Wood’s American Gothic depicts a farmer and his daughter in front of a Carpenter Gothic home, evoking images of an earlier generation. While some saw it as a satirical critique of rural life, Wood intended the painting to offer a reassuring portrayal of traditional American values during the Great Depression.
