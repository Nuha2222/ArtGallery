Blue and Green Music
Georgia O'Keeffe
1919–1921
Oil on canvas
58.4 × 48.3 cm (23 × 19 in.)

Around 1920, Georgia O'Keeffe explored translating music into visual art, as seen in Blue and Green Music, where colors and forms evoke both nature and sound. Influenced by Vasily Kandinsky’s ideas on art and music, O'Keeffe aimed to achieve pure visual expression without literary references.
